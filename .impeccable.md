## Design Context

### Users
Students and early-career individuals (16–25) at a crossroads — unsure what to study, pursue, or build. They use this in moments of genuine uncertainty: late at night, early morning before school, during a gap year. The context is personal and slightly anxious. They want clarity, not cheerleading.

### Brand Personality
**Precise. Guiding. Transformative.**
Not a motivational app. Not a chatbot. A structured intelligence that understands you better than you do. Tone is calm, minimal, exact — like a brilliant mentor who speaks only when it matters. No superlatives, no emojis, no exclamation marks. Every word earns its place.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark — users engage in focused, private moments. Not a coffee-shop app.
- **Palette**: Deep black (`#000`), warm cream (`#DEDBC8` / `#E1E0CC`), very dark card surfaces (`#0A0A0A`, `#101010`, `#212121`). No blue accents. No neon. No gradients on text.
- **Typography**: Almarai (global, weights 300–800) paired with Spectral italic (accent only). Spectral replaces Instrument Serif — more precise, less romantic.
- **Motion**: Slow, deliberate. Pull-up reveals, scroll-linked opacity, smooth step transitions. No bounce. No elastic. Ease-out-expo for everything.
- **Visual metaphors**: Navigation, clarity, structure. Prisma refracts confusion into clear directions — like light through a prism.
- **Anti-references**: No dashboard "grid of cards with icon + heading + body". No gradient hero text. No rounded card grids that look like any SaaS landing page.

### Design Principles
1. **Precision over warmth** — every element must feel intentional. Generous whitespace, tight typography, nothing decorative that doesn't serve meaning.
2. **Cinematic restraint** — the video hero, noise textures, and large type create drama without gimmicks. Keep it understated.
3. **Form as conversation** — onboarding inputs should feel like dialogue, not data entry. Labels are sentences, not field names.
4. **Cream on black is the palette** — do not introduce blue, purple, or any other accent. The cream IS the accent.
5. **Motion signals intelligence** — the generating screen, step transitions, and scroll effects should feel like a system thinking, not a website animating.
